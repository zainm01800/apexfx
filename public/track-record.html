How this record stays honest
  • Logged before the outcome. Every verdict — direction, confidence, entry/stop/target — is recorded the moment it's made. Nothing is backfilled.
  • Losses are never deleted. Wins and losses are shown side by side. No cherry-picking.
  • Graded automatically. A scheduled job decides win/loss purely from whether the market price reached the stop or take-profit we stated — the score isn't hand-edited.
